Checklists & Tours · Page Specification

Checklist Detail

Read-only view of a completed Checklist instance — the full record of what was checked, by whom, and what it turned up.

Purpose

Reached from Checklist list once a checklist's status is Complete. Distinct from Active checklist, which handles Not Started/In Progress — this page never accepts new input.

Data shown

FieldSourceNotes
Template & assignmentChecklist.template, Checklist.assigned_to
TimingChecklist.started_at / completed_at
Item resultsChecklist.item_results → ChecklistItemResultEach item's recorded result, including Door Check retry history and Verify Task rejection reasons.
Linked ticketsChecklistItemResult.linked_ticketShown inline against the item that raised them, linking to Ticket detail.
Nested Location-Based ChecksNested Checklist instancesShown expandable under their parent item rather than as separate top-level entries.

Actions

ActionGatingBehavior
Open a linked ticketview_incidents not required for tickets themselvesNavigates to Ticket detail.
Open the location of a Location-Based CheckNoneNavigates to Location detail.

No edit actions exist on this screen — a completed checklist instance is immutable, consistent with it being an Activity Log-eligible historical record.

States & edge cases

  • Item with no note or ticket — displayed plainly with just its result; not every failed Door Check or rejected Verify Task necessarily produced a ticket.
  • Very long checklists — item list is virtualized/paginated rather than rendered in full at once on lower-end devices.

Mobile vs. desktop

Mobile: item results as stacked cards, one per item. Desktop: a denser list/table layout with results visible without expanding each item individually.
