Owners & Contacts · Page Specification

Owner / Contact List

Every Contact known to the marina — boat owners, authorized users, lessees, and anyone who's simply called or texted in.

Purpose

Reached from the Owners & Contacts top-level nav item (note the nav groups Boats separately, but Contacts underlies both — see Terminology). A Contact isn't necessarily a User; most entries here never sign into the app at all.

Data shown

FieldSourceNotes
NameContact.nameRequires view_owner; nameless contacts (name = null) show a "needs a name" indicator instead. Gating extended here from its literal "slip/boat owner" wording in Permissions to cover Contacts generally, for consistency with how the same two permissions gate Contact info everywhere else it appears (Boat, Location, Active Call Panel, etc.).
Phone / emailContact.phone / emailRequires view_contact, additionally to view_owner.
Linked boatsBoat.owners / authorized_users containing this ContactShown as a short tag/count, not full detail.

Actions

ActionGatingBehavior
Open a contactview_ownerNavigates to Owner/Contact detail — or, for a nameless contact, into the name prompt & merge dialog first.
Add a contactedit_owner_contactCreates a new Contact directly (as opposed to one auto-created by an inbound call/text).
Search / filterSame as viewingBy name or phone.

States & edge cases

  • No view_owner — the list and the Owners & Contacts nav item are effectively unreachable in any useful form.
  • Merged contact (merged_into set) — excluded from this list entirely; only the canonical target record appears, per Data Model — Contact.
  • Nameless contact — included in the list with a distinct visual treatment, so staff can spot ones that still need a name/merge decision.

Mobile vs. desktop

Mobile: single searchable list. Desktop: sortable table, with the phone/email columns present only for users holding view_contact.
