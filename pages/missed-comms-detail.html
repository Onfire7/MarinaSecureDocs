Missed Calls / Unread SMS Detail

What opens when the floating Missed Comms Badge is tapped — every missed call and unread text in one combined view, including voicemail playback.

Purpose

The Missed Comms Badge (see Dashboard) is a floating, always-visible count of missed calls plus unread SMS, per Role Workflows. This screen is what it opens into — a combined worklist for catching up, not a permanent home for call/text history (that's Comms Home). Its two halves are gated independently: view_calls for missed calls, view_sms for unread texts, same as the sections they mirror on Comms Home.

Data shown

FieldRequiresSourceNotes
Missed callsview_callsCall.missed = trueContact, line, time; voicemail playback inline if Call.voicemail_url is set.
Unread textsview_smsSMSThread.unread = trueContact, line, last message preview.

Actions

ActionGatingBehavior
Play a voicemailview_callsInline playback of Call.voicemail_url.
Open a missed callview_callsExpands inline the same way as on Comms Home — matched contact, notes, recording/transcript if enabled.
Call backplace_callsOpens New call dialog pre-filled with the contact/number. Independent of view_calls — a user could plausibly call back from the badge count alone without full history access, though in practice the two are usually held together.
Open an unread threadview_smsNavigates to SMS thread; marks SMSThread.unread = false on open.
Text backplace_callsOpens the relevant SMS thread composer directly.

States & edge cases

  • No view_calls — the missed-calls half is omitted; a badge count sourced only from unread SMS still shows if the user holds view_sms.
  • No view_sms — same treatment, mirrored, for the unread-texts half.
  • Neither permission — the badge itself has nothing to show and doesn't appear (see Dashboard).
  • Nothing missed or unread — empty state; the badge itself wouldn't be showing a count in this case, so this is mostly reached via direct navigation rather than the badge.
  • Marking read is one-way from here — opening a thread clears its unread flag; there's no "mark unread" action on this screen (that's a property of engaging with the thread, not something manually toggled).
  • Recording/transcription disabled — same as elsewhere, that section is simply omitted, not shown empty.

Mobile vs. desktop

Mobile: opens full-screen from the badge tap. Desktop: opens as a popover anchored to the badge rather than taking over the screen, consistent with it being a quick worklist rather than a destination.
